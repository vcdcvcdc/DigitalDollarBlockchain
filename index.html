Testing DDBC:

Step 1

Establish a Connection with an EVM Wallet: Initiate your DDBC testing by connecting with an EVM Wallet using the details below:

EVM RPC: https://ddbc.dev
ChainID: 7777
Token: Karma
EVM Explorer: https://evm.explorer.ddbc.dev
Establish a Connection with a Cosmos Wallet:
To connect with a Cosmos Wallet, use the information provided below:

COSMOS RPC: http://3.137.200.25:26657 (visit this link, and it will auto-load in Keplr with a connected wallet)
Cosmos Explorer: https://ibc.explorer.ddbc.dev

Step 2

Navigate to https://frontend.ddbc.dev/login, link your wallet, and pause for 60 seconds.

Step 3

KYC Part 1 (Automated Upon Launch): For testing purposes, apply the following command:
curl -X POST "https://backend.ddbc.dev/api/v1/account/add/{address}
Note: Substitute {address} with your wallet address. If you prefer to avoid the command line, you can use https://reqbin.com/curl.

Step 4

KYC Part 2: Visit http://kyc.ddbc.dev and complete the KYC process.
Upon completion, you will be rewarded with additional Karma. Ensure your web3 wallet is linked to the network, refresh the page, and it will sign in with MetaMas

Step 5

Now you are ready to transact with Karma. Every transaction rewards both the sender and receiver with more Karma, ensuring you always have sufficient Karma to finalize a transaction.

Step 6

Initiate a contract. However, remember that it will not allow transactions until you execute the following command:
curl -X POST "https://backend.ddbc.dev/api/v1/account/add/{contract}
